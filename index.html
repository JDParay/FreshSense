Early Odor-Based Detection System

FreshSense uses an advanced odor sensor that can detect extremely small changes in the air—changes that usually appear hours or even days before mold, bacteria, or contamination become visible.

By identifying these early warning signs, farmers are given enough time to take action, adjust conditions, and prevent the spread of harmful microorganisms. This early detection greatly reduces the chances of losing large portions of the harvest.

Real-Time Alerts Through a Mobile App

The system is connected to an easy-to-use mobile application that sends real-time alerts directly to the farmer’s phone whenever something unusual is detected.

These notifications come with clear messages, color indicators, and simple explanations, making it easy for farmers to understand what might be happening in their grow rooms.

24/7 Automated Monitoring With Low Maintenance

FreshSense provides continuous, around-the-clock monitoring, ensuring that the farm is being checked at all times, situations such as the farmer being away or asleep.

The device works quietly in the background and requires very little maintenance, removing the need for constant manual checking.
